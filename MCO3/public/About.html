About BookMyLab

BookMyLab is an innovative platform designed to streamline the process of reserving lab slots for students.
With BookMyLab, students can easily view available lab times, make reservations, and ensure
they have the necessary resources and space to complete their projects and studies.
Our mission is to provide an efficient and user-friendly system that meets the needs of students and faculty alike.

Our Features

Easy lab slot reservations
Real-time availability checks
User account management
Detailed reservation history
Mobile-friendly interface
